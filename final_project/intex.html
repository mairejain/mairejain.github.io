Introduction

I am a Detroit native (born and raised). My family is from Italy (Reggio Calabira, specifically). I speak Italian, but only a slang dialect called 'Calabrese.' A little known fact about me is that I am a washed-up rapper. For those of you who don't know what this means, I had a record deal and it went flat. I made the deadly mistake of signing all three contracts with the same entity, which meant that this company controlled everything, including sub-contract negotiations. I was slated to open up for Mariah Carey and Slick Rick, but my management company rejected the deal (something I would not have done). I have not opened up for may super-famous people, but here's the short list:

  • Markey-Mark and the Funky Bunch
  • MC Eiht & Compton's Most Wanted
  • MC Breed

I have met many famous people, including superstars in both rap and R&B. Here's a list of those people:

  • Babyface
  • Kid & Play
  • Kool Moe Dee
  • Aaron Hall (from Guy)
  • Betty Wright
  • Kurtis Blow (he was my producer)

Hope you have enjoyed getting to learn about me. I don't share this information with may people. And, yes, I do miss performing. (And giving out autographs!)
